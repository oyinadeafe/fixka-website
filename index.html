FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Fixka and car insurance? ▾
Car insurance cover damages from accidents, theft, or third-party liability. Fixka covers vehicle maintenance and mechanical repairs; the everyday costs that keep your car running smoothly. Think of Fixka as a maintenance subscription, not an accident policy. Many drivers use both together for complete peace of mind.
What exactly does Fixka cover? ▾
Fixka covers routine maintenance services and mechanical repairs depending on your chosen plan. Essential covers basic servicing. Pro adds brake work, suspension, electrical, and towing. Elite covers everything including clutch, drivetrain, timing belt, and advanced components. See the full breakdown in the pricing section above.
How do I get my vehicle repaired with Fixka? ▾
Once subscribed, you receive a digital Fixka ID. When you need a repair, visit any Fixka-certified garage, show your Fixka ID, and the garage handles everything. Fixka pays the workshop directly; no cash needed, no reimbursement delays. You can also use the Fixka app to find your nearest certified garage and track your service in real time.
